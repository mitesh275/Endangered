Endangered Species


Species that are on the verge of extinction due to their decreasing population on account of enviroment or predatory factors

are referred to as Endangered



House Sparrow

Birds, our chirpy feathered friends, who have amazed us by their aeodynamics and exotic colour, are under great threat. There are 9,934 species of birds that are existing in the world today, out of which, a staggering 1,313 (13%) are threatened under extinction. House Sparrow is one of them.


Sparrows, though tiny, are very sensitive and strongly immune birds, and their sudden disappearance as sentinals or as ecological indicators is a warning to humans about impending enviromental hazards

Reasons for the dislocation of Sparrow


Non-availabliltiy of tiny insects as food due to the loss of vegitation

The excessive use of mosquito repellents

Concrete architectures with no nesting sites for sparrow

Ai-conditioning which leaves no entry or exit points for feeding sparrow nesting
